Briefs and Bouquets

Whispering Falls #4

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

Love or Law? A Chicago lawyer's heart tangles with a laid-back charmer in the eye of the storm...

In the bustling city of Chicago, Willow Davies is a driven lawyer on the cusp of achieving her long-awaited partnership. But when her younger sister's wedding calls her to the quaint town of Whispering Falls, Massachusetts, Willow finds herself torn between her career ambitions and the pull of family obligations.

As the Maid of Honor, Willow is unexpectedly paired with the charming and laid-back Best Man, Rowan McCarthy. Through a whirlwind of wedding festivities and a serendipitous scavenger hunt, Willow begins to question her structured approach to life and the dreams she's been chasing.

But when a hurricane strikes, trapping Willow in Whispering Falls, she finds herself seeking shelter in Rowan's home. As the storm rages outside, Willow must navigate the tempest within her own heart, confronting the growing attraction to Rowan and the doubts about her chosen path.

Will she choose the life she's always planned, or will she embrace the unexpected love that's been brewing amidst the storm?
