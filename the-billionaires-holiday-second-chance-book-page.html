The Billionaire's Holiday Second Chance

She’s the one woman he can’t negotiate with. He’s the one mistake she never forgot.

When billionaire dealmaker, Jamison Randolf, unexpectedly takes over BrightCo, he comes face-to-face with the woman he walked away from—and the chemistry that never cooled.

But when a leaked memo threatens to destroy both their reputations, Jade must decide whether to trust him again.

And this Christmas, Jamison will have to prove that love isn’t just part of the deal… It’s the only thing he wants.
