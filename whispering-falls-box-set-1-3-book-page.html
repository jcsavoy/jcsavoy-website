Books 1-3

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

Escape to Whispering Falls with this heartwarming collection of sweet romances.

Where love blooms in unexpected ways, second chances linger in the air, and small-town charm is always in season. This boxed set includes the first three full-length novels in the Whispering Falls Sweet Romance series—perfect for fans of cozy towns, feel-good love stories, and emotionally satisfying journeys.

Inheriting Love: When grumpy New York graphic designer Steven Hillman inherits a rundown house, the last thing he expects is Danika Walters—Miss Small-Town Sunshine herself.

Summertime Sonata: World-renowned violinist Anna Avery returns home to honor her late mother—and ends up face-to-face with the man she once loved.

Rebuilding Hearts: Jack Delaney never expected to return to Whispering Falls—or to raise his daughter alone. But when he reconnects with Sophie Turner, the girl he once loved, old feelings resurface fast.

If you enjoy small-town settings, layered characters, and sweet, slow-burn romances with plenty of heart, you’ll love this collection of Whispering Falls stories.
