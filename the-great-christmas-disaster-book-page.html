The Great Christmas Disaster

Love Only #1

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

She brought forty-seven contingency plans. He wasn't one of them.

Olivia Denton has a color-coded binder, a tackle box of emergency supplies, and forty-seven laminated contingency protocols. When she volunteers to coordinate Cliffhaven's Christmas Festival, she is ready for anything.

Except Noah Hampton standing in his late uncle's tree lot looking annoyingly competent in flannel.

Noah is only in town to close out his uncle's final tree season. Back in Bangor by January. He is not getting attached to a woman who names every tree on the lot, inspects pine needles with a magnifying glass, and falls backward into a noble fir within twenty minutes of meeting him.

Then an ice storm hits. Then a blizzard. Then she blows the town's power grid for the second time in fifteen years, and he finds her sitting in the dark with a rain poncho and a box of ornament hooks.

Some disasters you plan for. Some you fall into headfirst.

A clean, sweet rom-com set in Cliffhaven, Maine, where the bakery owner won't stop calling it a date and the hardware store owner reviews every situation with a single word: "Yep."
