Matchmaker's Dilemma

Whispering Falls #8

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

When love is all about the perfect match, what happens when you become the one who’s off-limits?

Shannon Cook is a matchmaker with a rule: she doesn’t date her clients. Ever. With her one-of-a-kind algorithm and a reputation as unshakable as her resolve, she’s made a business out of finding everyone else’s perfect match in the picturesque town of Whispering Falls. Her own love life? Well, let’s just say it’s a sacrifice she’s more than willing to make.

Enter Tyler Scott—a charming, commitment-phobic playboy who’d rather be anywhere else than in Shannon’s office. But thanks to his father’s ultimatum (find a fiancée or kiss the family business goodbye), Tyler has no choice but to use Shannon’s matchmaking services. Trouble is, every woman Shannon sends his way is a flop. Miserably. And no one is more surprised than Shannon when Tyler’s attention starts shifting from the dates...to her.

Tyler may be a disaster when it comes to romance, but he’s good at one thing—getting what he wants. And now, what he wants is Shannon. The only problem? Falling for a client could ruin everything Shannon’s worked for. But as mismatches, witty banter, and awkwardly adorable encounters pile up, she starts wondering if maybe, just maybe, some rules were made to be broken.

Matchmaker’s Dilemma is a swoon-worthy romance about finding love where you least expect it—and realizing that sometimes, the perfect match is the one that defies all your algorithms.
