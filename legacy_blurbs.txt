AMAZON BLURBS FOR LEGACY BILLIONAIRES

Book 1: The Billionaire’s Stand-In Fiancée
What happens when a billionaire’s plan for a fake fiancée turns into a very real problem—called love?  Billionaire Adrian Cole can negotiate billion-dollar deals, but convincing his sharp-tongued assistant to pose as his fiancée for one weekend may be his riskiest venture yet.  Mindy Ellis agrees to the bonus and the challenge, not the butterflies, but somewhere between the contract and the champagne toasts, their act starts to feel dangerously real.  A sparkling, laugh-out-loud romantic comedy of fake rings, real feelings, and two people discovering that love doesn’t follow the fine print.


Book 2: The Billionaire’s Hidden Truth


He went undercover to save his company, never expecting to lose his heart.  Billionaire executive Holden Ramsey has everything—except answers. When Cole Industries’ flagship stores start failing, he trades his penthouse for a name tag and a retail uniform, determined to uncover what’s really happening on the front lines.  Assistant manager Skylar Hayes thinks she’s training a transfer from Chicago, not mentoring one of the most powerful men in Manhattan. Her sharp insights and quick wit captivate him, but when a late-night coffee turns into something deeper, Holden’s double life grows harder to hide. As secrets unravel and trust is tested, Skylar must decide if love built on deception can survive the truth.  A feel-good billionaire romance filled with humor, heart, and second chances, where the biggest risk isn’t going undercover, it’s falling in love.


Book 3: The Billionaire’s Stolen Heart


He’s guarding his career. She’s guarding her secret. Neither expects to lose their heart.  Forensic accountant Kelsey Sanders has one mission: uncover the truth about missing funds at Cole Industries. Her assignment? Go undercover as the new executive assistant to the company’s brilliant—and infuriatingly guarded—CFO, Preston Weldon.  Preston is still rebuilding his reputation after being burned at his last job, and trust doesn’t come easily. When he’s told to bring a date to a high-stakes investor gala, Kelsey steps in to play the part, and the chemistry between them becomes impossible to ignore.  She’s there to expose the truth. He’s determined to protect it. But when secrets collide, someone’s bound to lose more than their reputation… they just might lose their heart.  Flirty banter, sizzling chemistry, and a twisty mystery make The Billionaire’s Stolen Heart an irresistible read for fans of smart, feel-good romance.


Book 4: The Billionaire’s Big Heart

Sometimes the richest hearts are found where the signal fades.  When billionaire COO Drake Langford escapes New York for his secluded Maine cabin, he’s searching for quiet. That means no meetings, no headlines, and no expectations. What he doesn’t expect is a car in a ditch, a camera-wielding stranger, and a spark that feels more real than anything in his boardroom life.  Madison Linfield came to the woods to rebuild her career and her courage, not to fall for a man who seems as grounded as he is mysterious. Between candlelit conversations, misty hikes, and laughter that feels like coming home, she starts to see the man behind the guarded smile.  But when Madison discovers Drake’s true identity, both must decide what matters more—the world he built or the love that’s rebuilding them both.  A sweet billionaire romance about second chances, quiet courage, and finding love where you least expect it.


Book 5: The Billionaire’s Holiday Second Chance

She’s the one woman he can’t negotiate with. He’s the one mistake she never forgot.  When billionaire dealmaker, Jamison Randolf, unexpectedly takes over BrightCo, he comes face-to-face with the woman he walked away from—and the chemistry that never cooled.  But when a leaked memo threatens to destroy both their reputations, Jade must decide whether to trust him again.  And this Christmas, Jamison will have to prove that love isn’t just part of the deal… It’s the only thing he wants.


Book 6: The Billionaire’s Surprise Son

When fate hands him a son, it also hands him a chance at love.  When a woman shows up in his Manhattan office with a little boy who looks just like him, billionaire Grant Dupont’s orderly world shatters.  Heather James never wanted anything from him—except the truth for her nephew. But one unexpected meeting turns into days of shared responsibilities, new routines, and a connection none of them saw coming.  As Grant steps into a role he never imagined and Heather fights feelings she never expected, one question becomes impossible to ignore:  Can a surprise twist of fate become the start of a real family?


Book 7: The Billionaire’s Matchmaker

A billionaire boss, a matchmaking plan, and a heart she never meant to risk.  Lilly Linwood has survived ten months as assistant to tech-genius Dillon Malone—no small feat when your boss is brilliant, brooding, and hopelessly single. So when his family demands he bring a date to his father’s retirement party, Lilly does what any responsible assistant would do:  She volunteers to find him one.  What starts as a simple matchmaking favor soon spirals into practice dates, unexpected chemistry, and one very inconvenient truth: some matches can’t be manufactured.  Now the party is approaching, feelings are becoming complicated, and Lilly is discovering the hardest part of love isn’t finding the right person…  It’s admitting it might be him.  In this clean, heartfelt billionaire romance, sparks fly, loyalties tangle, and love arrives in the very last place they expect.


Book 8: The Billionaire’s Guarded Heart

Grumpy vs Sunshine. A whisper of danger. And a guarded heart about to meet its match.  Amy Young has one dream: to pull off the biggest event of her career.  Declan Carter has one job: to keep her safe.  She radiates warmth, joy, and impossible hope, while he’s all sharp edges, silent watchfulness, and a heart wrapped in steel.  When a mysterious threat puts the gala at risk, their worlds collide in late-night planning sessions, a reluctant partnership, and a chemistry neither of them is prepared for. Amy sees the good he’s forgotten he has. Declan sees the danger she refuses to acknowledge.  But protecting her might mean breaking the one rule he’s lived by since the day he lost everything— never get close.
