Inheriting Love

Whispering Falls #1

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

Small-town girl meets grumpy big city boy, and those two do not see eye-to-eye.

Whispering Falls is more than just a postcard-perfect town with a rich history. It's where unexpected encounters can lead to life-changing sparks. Danika Walters, the life of the local history scene, is all about community vibes and keeping the town's old-school charm alive. She's got this knack for making everyone feel like they're home.

Steven Hillman is a graphic designer straight out of the NYC hustle. Life throws him a curveball, however, when he inherits his uncle's rundown home in Whispering Falls. He's a grumpy loner when it comes to relationships, which is pretty much the opposite of Danika's outgoing personality.

Having to take time off from his job in New York for a small town puts his love for going it alone to the test when he meets the intriguing Danika.

The two are thrown together by fate with a side of fresh chemistry. They're both trying to do something great with the old house, but their plans are as mismatched as their personalities, making Steven rethink his lone-wolf ways and lighting up some of Danika's own pushed-aside dreams.

INHERITING LOVE dives into a down-to-earth love that unfolds in the laid-back lanes of Whispering Falls, where falling leaves and falling in love remind us that the best things we inherit aren't just houses or antiques, but the chance to find true love.
