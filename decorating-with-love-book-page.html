Decorating With Love

Whispering Falls #5

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

In the whisper of love's design, can a faux engagement lead to true romance?

In the cozy town of Whispering Falls, Massachusetts, Joy Fletcher's dreams and designs are the heart of her struggling interior design firm. When the opportunity to decorate the home of the town's new doctor arises, Joy sees it as her chance to prove her talent and secure her business' future.

Dr. Cameron Browning leaves the hustle of Boston for a quieter life in Whispering Falls in order to provide a stable home for his son, Sam. Committed to protecting him from any more loss, Cameron has kept his relationships light and his heart guarded—until a blast from the past threatens to unravel the life he's carefully built.

Brittany Welling's unexpected arrival in Whispering Falls, aiming to rekindle her relationship with Cameron, prompts a panicked lie from him that he's engaged to Joy Fletcher who reluctantly agrees to the charade to save her crucial commission.

But as the pretend engagement becomes public gossip, Cameron and Joy find themselves entangled in a web of deception, community scrutiny, and unexpected feelings.

Set in charming Whispering Falls, Massachusetts—a town with a feel-good sense of community that will make you want to visit time and again.
