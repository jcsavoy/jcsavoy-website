Blueprint of the Heart

Whispering Falls #12

Buy on Amazon Read the Blurb

Sometimes love needs a new foundation.

When Boston architect Charlie Reynolds returns to Whispering Falls to restore a crumbling Victorian into a pottery studio, she's focused on one thing: proving she can rebuild her career on her own terms. She doesn’t expect to clash with rugged, small-town contractor Rider Summers—especially not over bricks, budgets, or bakery choices.

But between floured cheeks, snowstorm strolls, and Sunday sourdough, something starts to shift. As walls come down and sparks begin to fly, Charlie must decide if the future she’s designing still leads back to the city—or if the real blueprint of her heart has been unfolding here all along.
